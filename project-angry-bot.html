Angry Bot Simulator

Meet Aemili! She is an Agent with Emotional Intelligence. She won't bite she may need a few empathetic utterances to assuage her frustrations... or not. This chatbot is one of four conceptual models trained to produce novel emotion specific responses to user input (in this case highly intense, unpleasant emotional states [i.e., angry, inense, frustrated]). She was developed using Python and TensorFlow libraries and she was modeled using the Seq2Seq Neural Machine Translation architecture.

Due to GPU limitations she was trained on the Google Colab development platform. She is trained to generate novel responses to novel user utterances. She does this by computing a series of condiational probabilities (encoding) and constructing an utterance based upon the computed probability distribution (decoder).

As you can see here, some of Aemili's responses are not so nice. She was trained using a custom made data set (see Research and Development Section) as well as a general conversational dialog database (Cornell Movie Dialog Corpus). For more details on this project. Please follow the link below.
